EmphAI (GreenLoop AI) is an AI-driven, sustainability-focused platform designed to detect perishable overstock before expiry and intelligently redirect it into circular economy channels such as biofuel production, composting, or sustainable packaging.

The project focuses on leveraging data intelligence and exploratory analysis to enable proactive decision-making, reduce food waste, and quantify environmental impact for large-scale retailers.

EmphAI introduces a data-driven circular economy infrastructure that identifies spoilage risks early and enables sustainable rerouting of goods.
